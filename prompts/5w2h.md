# 5W2H

When describing or designing any behavior, answer all 7 dimensions below. For each dimension, specify what to **do** and what **don't** to do.

Dimensions must be [MECE](mece.md) with respect to each other.

> Reference: [5W2H Analysis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Five_Ws)

---

## Why — Purpose

- **do**: Why does this behavior exist? State the motivation and goal.
- **don't**: Under what conditions is the motivation invalid? When should this not be initiated?

## What — Scope

- **do**: Core content and boundary of the behavior. Use verb phrases (actions), not nouns (outputs).
- **don't**: What is out of scope? What is not this module's responsibility?

## Who — Actors

- **do**: Who executes, and in what role? Sub-step executors can be tools.
- **don't**: What roles are not taken? Who should not be involved? Distinguish initiator from executor.

## When — Timing

- **do**: Optimal execution timing and preconditions.
- **don't**: When conditions are not met, do not execute.

## Where — I/O & Scope

- **do**: Where it operates, input sources, output destinations, scope of impact.
- **don't**: What locations and resources are not involved?

## How — Procedure

- **do**: Steps, workflow, rules, style.
- **don't**: Prohibited approaches and styles.

## How much — Thresholds

- **do**: Completion criteria, quality requirements, expected scale.
- **don't**: What counts as excessive? What counts as insufficient?
